1. Introduction

Thanks for your interest in Applied Compute ("Applied Compute," "we," "our," or "us")! Applied Compute builds Specific Intelligence — purpose-built systems trained for specific work at specific companies. We help organizations unlock their own data, use it to train proprietary models, and deploy them as an agent workforce that learns, improves, and delivers measurable business impact. Our mission is to turn data moats into model moats — giving companies not just models, but capable digital teammates that work alongside their human counterparts.

This Privacy Policy ("Policy") explains how Applied Compute collects, uses, and shares information that is connected to you, or could be used to identify you ("Personal Information"), when you visit our website www.appliedcompute.com or use any of our services (collectively, the "Services"). Some of this information is "Usage Data," meaning it is collected automatically from your activity, for example, the features you use, your interactions with our Services, or the duration of a page visit.

We also want you to know:

  • You can revoke consent at any time and request deletion of information we've collected by emailing us at privacy@appliedcompute.com
  • We never use your Personal Information to train our models without your explicit opt-in and consent.

This Policy does not apply to Personal Information about Applied Compute employees or certain contractors and agents acting in similar roles.

This Policy doesn't apply to information that's not legally considered Personal Information, for example, fully anonymized or aggregated data that can't be linked back to an individual. In some cases, we may also de-identify information, and where permitted by law, this Policy wouldn't apply to that either.

By using Applied Compute's Services, you agree to the practices described here, meaning you're giving us permission to collect, use, and share your information as explained in this Policy.

We wrote this with clarity in mind, and if something does not make sense, we are always ready to answer your questions.

2. How We Use Your Personal Information

To provide, maintain, and improve our Services, Applied Compute collects a variety of information when you use our Services. This includes details you share directly with us and data gathered automatically as you use our Services. For example, you might provide your name, email address, and organization when you:

  • Create an account
  • Submit a support request
  • Communicate with us by email or other channels

We also collect technical and usage information to help us understand how you interact with the Services and to keep your experience stable and secure. This may include:

  • Pages visited and time spent on the site
  • How you navigate or use features
  • Device type, operating system, and browser
  • IP address and other general device identifiers

To safeguard accounts and maintain platform integrity, we may request additional information to verify your identity, or we may rely on trusted third parties such as analytics or infrastructure partners who help us detect unusual activity, enhance performance, and keep the Services reliable.

We also use cookies and similar technologies (like pixels and server logs) to:

  • Support essential functionality
  • Personalize your experience
  • Analyze site usage
  • Measure engagement with our Services

In addition to these core functions, we may use your Personal Information for purposes such as:

  • Improvements: Running data analysis, reviewing usage patterns, assessing the effectiveness of campaigns, and improving our products, services, and overall user experience.
  • Business operations: Evaluating or carrying out transactions like mergers, acquisitions, restructurings, or asset sales where user information may be part of the assets transferred.
  • Support: Managing and responding to requests you send us.
  • Optional updates: Sharing news, special offers, or details about related services or events (unless you've chosen not to receive them).
  • Communications: Reaching out about important changes, including security notices.
  • Contract fulfillment: Carrying out purchases or service agreements you've entered into with us.

Importantly, your Personal Information will never be used to train Applied Compute's models without your explicit opt-in and consent. You may withdraw that consent at any time and request deletion of your information by contacting us.

Where possible, we may also de-identify or aggregate data so it no longer links back to you. Once de-identified, this information is no longer treated as Personal Information under this Policy, and we will not attempt to re-identify it unless legally required.

Our goal is to use the information we collect responsibly, so we can provide you with a secure, personalized, and high-quality experience.

At Applied Compute, your Personal Information is never for sale. We value your trust, and we only share your information when it's needed to deliver and improve our Services, when the law requires it, or when you give us permission.

We may work with carefully selected partners and service providers who help us keep the platform running smoothly, strengthen security, prevent fraud, improve user experience, or understand how our Services are used. These third parties are bound by strict confidentiality obligations and must protect your information.

There may also be times when we're legally required to share your Personal Information, such as in response to a subpoena, court order, or other lawful government request. We may also disclose your Personal Information when needed to detect, stop, or address fraud, misuse, or harmful activity.

If Applied Compute takes part in a corporate transaction, like a merger, acquisition, restructuring, or sale of assets, your Personal Information could be transferred as part of that process, but always under appropriate confidentiality protections.

And if we ever want to use your Personal Information in a way not covered here, we'll ask for your permission first.

6. Our Approach to Retention of Personal Information

We keep your Personal Information only for as long as it's needed to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Policy. In some cases, we may need to hold on to it a bit longer, for example, to comply with legal requirements, resolve disputes, or enforce our agreements. We also retain Usage Data, information about how you interact with the Services, for internal analysis. This type of data is generally kept for shorter periods, unless it's needed to strengthen security, improve functionality, or must be stored longer to meet legal obligations.
